Projects

FILNYC

The International Book Fair of the City of New York arose as an initiative of the City University of New York Mexican Studies Institute to promote the talent of writers who carry out their poetic and literary production in Spanish. The institute needed a page to present the event, display images, and give information to writers and the general public. Using HTML, CSS, and javascript, it was possible to obtain a page that is quick to access and responsive to adapt its presentation depending on the screen of the user's device. This allowed the institute to expand its outreach and more easily provide information to its followers.

Provenance Restoration Inc.

The boss of a group of men decided to retire and give the restoration company rights to them. The new owners needed a website to display their works to potential clients when promoting their business. Different languages and frameworks such as html, css, javascript, Google Maps API, bootstrap, jquery and images, provided by the owners, were used for this project.
The owners now have a gallery on the page that they use to show their clients the before and after of their work which has caused them to increase their number of clients compared to the amount they had before using a page.
